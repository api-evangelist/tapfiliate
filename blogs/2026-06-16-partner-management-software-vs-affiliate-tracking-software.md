This guide distinguishes between partner management platforms and affiliate tracking tools. Affiliate tracking focuses on attribution, commissions, clicks, conversions, and payout automation, while partner management emphasizes deal registration and channel revenue, helping businesses select tools based on partner ecosystem complexity.
